    Groove of the Abyss is set to take listeners on a mind-bending journey with their debut album, set for release in early 2025. This groundbreaking project encapsulates the band’s signature blend of dark funkadelic sounds, inspired by the mystical energy of their hometown, New Orleans. “This new album channels the soul of New Orleans, blending its funk roots with its mystical essence,” said Rolling Stone. Tracks like The Devil’s Boogie and Voodoo Funk Machine bring pulsating grooves and spellbinding rhythms, while songs like The Moth to the Flame and Electric Throne delve into psychedelic territories, weaving layers of sound that transport listeners into a cosmic dancefloor. Other standout tracks, including Soulfire Sins and Orange Peel Inferno, showcase the band’s ability to merge haunting melodies with high-energy, bass-heavy funk. With vivid instrumentation, eerie undertones, and lyrics that echo the mysteries of the unknown, Groove of the Abyss captures the essence of Freak Harmonics’ vision: a revival of groove with an unearthly twist. This new album invites listeners to immerse themselves in a soundscape that feels both nostalgic and otherworldly, carving out their place as musical innovators and keepers of New Orleans’ rich spiritual legacy.
